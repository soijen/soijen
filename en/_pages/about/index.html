ABOUT

Our Inspiration

We are Jess and Eduardo. We come from very different backgrounds, but together we share a love for the outdoors, especially for Chilean Patagonia.

We draw our inspiration from the Selk’nam – the indigenous people of southern Patagonia. The Selk’nam were nomadic hunters, traveling by foot across the desolate landscapes at the end of the world. When they crossed paths, they celebrated with unique footraces called káiyik, sprinting around lakes or racing to the tops of hills. The winner was the sóijen – or champion runner.

We are inspired by this collective passion for the outdoors, especially when it comes to running on trails and in the mountains. Through Sóijen we want to share our experiences in Patagonia, giving you the most complete information so you can start your own journey.

Welcome to Sóijen, your resource for Patagonia unexplored.
